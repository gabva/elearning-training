Inland Rules of the Road

REFERENCE: U.S. Coast Guard, Commandant Instruction M16672.2C

The material of this course is of the U.S Coast Guard's Rules of the Road in compliance with U.S. Regulations.


LESSON OBJECTIVE: This course will give the student a basic understanding of the Inland Rules of Navigation. These rules have been Coast Guard approved according to the instruction listed above and U.S. Law.
